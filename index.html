How The App Works?

How does it work?
  • Upon selecting the destination within the park me up app, a real time display of the parking slots available for you to choose from comes up. Click on book now and it gets reserved for you. Drive, arrive, scan the barcode received in the app at the entrance and park. It’s that simple!
How are the parking charges calculated?
  • A minimum amount is selected on the basis of the type of vehicle and the location selected. Charges are computed depending on the number of hours the parking slot is occupied and the entire payment is completed by scanning the barcode scanner at the exit.
Would I get a refund on cancellation of my booking?
  • If the cancellation of booking is done half an hour prior to the time slot booked, the entire booking amount is refunded to the users. For cancellations done later than that, the amount is forfeited.
What if I arrive early or stay late?
  • Depending on the number of hours you overstay, parking amount would be computed at the time of checkout and payment would need to be processed before exiting. The computation of the extra charges would be done on a hourly basis as well.
